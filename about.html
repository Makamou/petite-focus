About Me

Welcome — I’m Moubarak Akamou, a photographer and front-end developer dedicated to capturing meaningful moments and crafting refined digital experiences.

With a longstanding passion for photography, I specialize in portraits, landscapes, and candid lifestyle imagery, focusing on authentic storytelling and timeless composition. My work seeks to preserve the essence of connection and emotion in every frame.

In parallel, I bring over two years of experience as a front-end developer, building responsive and accessible web applications using JavaScript, React, HTML, CSS, and Tailwind. Currently pursuing a Master’s in Cloud Computing Systems, I am committed to delivering scalable, high-performance solutions that integrate technical precision with thoughtful design.

I view both photography and web development as mediums to convey clarity, narrative, and lasting impact. Thank you for visiting my gallery. I welcome opportunities to collaborate or connect.
